\begin{frame}

\frametitle{\centerline{$n$-bit Addition}}

\begin{center}

\vspace{\fill}

Given the bit-strings $x_{n-1}x_{n-2}\cdots x_0$ and $y_{n-1}y_{n-2}\cdots y_0$.

\pause

\bigskip

Show how to produce a bit-string $z_{n-1}z_{n-2}\cdots z_0$, \\ and a carry-
(overflow) bit $c_n$, such that

\pause

\bigskip

$$\sum_0^{n-1}z_i2^i + c_n= \sum_0^{n-1}x_i2^i + \sum_0^{n-1}y_i2^i$$

\end{center}

\end{frame}
